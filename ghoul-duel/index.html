Ghoul Duel — Bot Match
WASD / arrows or hold mouse to fly  ·  P pause  ·  M mute
An original fan tribute to the gameplay of Google’s 2018 “Great Ghoul Duel” Halloween Doodle — all art, code and sound here are original. Not affiliated with Google. Collect spirit flames, fly through a rival’s trail to steal it, bank at your base. You are the only human: 7 bots.
